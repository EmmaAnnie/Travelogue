Canmore, Alberta

The first trip on my bucket list is somewhere a little closer to home. Canmore is a great place to go if you want a mountain getaway while still being closer to home if you live in Alberta. I've always wanted to try staying in a cabin in the woods up in the mountains, with the amazing scenery and privacy when I want a little down time. Recently, a friend suggested that Canmore is the place to go if you want to find a nice Airbnb, where you can go on all sorts of tours and do a lot of sightseeing. The perfect thing about Canmore is that it doesn't get too busy, even around tourist season like other vacation spots might. The more I hear about Canmore, the more I realize it is the best place to try for my first time travelling as a young adult.
