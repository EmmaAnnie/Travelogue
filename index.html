Bucket List Destinations

My Future Travel Scrapbook

Canmore, Alberta

The first trip on my bucket list is somewhere a little closer to home. Canmore is a great place to go if you want a mountain getaway while still being closer to home if you live in Alberta. I've always wanted to try staying in a cabin in the woods up in the mountains, with the amazing scenery and privacy when I want a little down time. Recently, a friend suggested that Canmore is the place to go if you want to find a nice Airbnb, where you can go on all sorts of tours and do a lot of sightseeing. The perfect thing about Canmore is that it doesn't get too busy, even around tourist season like other vacation spots might. The more I hear about Canmore, the more I realize it is the best place to try for my first time travelling as a young adult.

Orlando, Florida (Universal Studios)

This destination sort of brings out my inner child. Since I was a kid, I've always enjoyed theme parks and being able to keep myself busy while on vacation. Universal Studios I feel is a great place to go on all kinds of adventures you usually only see on TV, including Jurassic Park, Transformers, and Harry Potter. The thing I would like most about this trip is that I wouldn't have to spend all my time in Universal Studios. There is so much more in Orlando and I wouldn't have to be stuck in one place, especially if I wanted some much needed down time. This trip may be something I wouldn't do until a few more years, but I still look forward to it.

Europe

This trip I couldn't limit to a single country. I've heard many great things about Europe and the destinations I could visit. One of my friends recently spent some time in Europe and told me all about the places he thought were amazing and would visit again, as well as the places he thought weren't as special. One splace that has great reviews is Switzerland. Switzerland is somewhere I feel would be a great place for sightseeing and many fun activities. The Swiss Alps in particular would be a great place for skiing, hiking, and snowboarding for those who love winter activities as well as amazing scenery for those who just love the view. This trip is definitely a far future trip, but still something I would love to try before my thirties.
